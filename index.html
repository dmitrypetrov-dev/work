The Money Machine

This machine consists of 3 Jars that serve as separate bank accounts for your kids money to be stored: Save, Spend, and Share jars.

The Save Jar

The Save Jar helps kids put aside money for their future and set quick and big goals to strive for.

The Spend Jar

The Spend Jar allows for kids to keep track of their debit card balance and review their purchase history.

The Share Jar

The Share Jar teaches kids generosity. Support charities you care about, with money automatically donated or as a one-time gift.

Simplified Budgeting

Visualize your money flow, making finances more engaging and easier to grasp than a standard bank statement.

What are Expectations?

Expectations are the tasks your kids are responsible for in your household. These are tasks you set and they will not be paid for.

Setting Expectations

This will give your kids a sense of self-worth and accomplishment while also understanding their role in the household economy.

What are Expenses?

Expenses are the items and experiences your kids are expected to pay for on their own. Think of this as their “wants” and not “needs”.

Types of Expenses

Expenses can be toys, games, extra clothes, in-app purchases, or social outings with their friends. More can be added as kids get older.

Set up Expenses

Set up expenses and empower your kids to plan for and make their own purchases. Build their independence and confidence.

What are Gigs?

Gigs are essentially small jobs that allow our young ones to earn money by creating value for others.

Create Gigs Together

Kids can work with the family to create Gigs they want to complete in order to make an impact and get paid for it.

Kids Request Gigs

With time, our young ones will find new opportunities to earn and request Gigs they want to do.

The Home Economy

Kids need to learn to get paid for the value they create so we created the 3E's Home Economy System to get them started.

The 3E’s of Home Economy

Kids will learn about Expectations (unpaid chores), Expenses (what they pay for), and Extra Pay (Action or Brain Gigs)

You Control Pay

As you help choose gigs you can set the amount and frequency so they can earn money each week.

Kids Get It!

No more begging for money. Within a month of using the 3E in-home economy system, your kids will stop asking you for money.
